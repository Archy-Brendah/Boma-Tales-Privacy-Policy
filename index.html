Boma Tales Privacy Policy

Global Privacy Policy (2026 Edition) | Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Boma Tales ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to preserving African heritage while protecting the privacy of families. This policy explains how we handle data for our "Little Explorers" and their parents.

2. Children’s Privacy (COPPA/GDPR-K Compliance)

Boma Tales is designed for children. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) from children.

  • Guardian Avatars: Choices are stored locally on your device.
  • Parental Gate: All sensitive actions (purchases/ads) are protected by a mathematical challenge intended for adults.

3. Data We Collect

  • Voice Recordings (Legacy Vault): Parent recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not upload these to our servers.
  • Payment Data: All transactions (M-PESA/Credit Cards) are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not see or store your PINs or card numbers.
  • Analytics: We use basic, anonymous data (e.g., "A story was read") to improve the app experience.

4. Permissions

  • Microphone: Used only when a parent initiates a "Legacy Recording."
  • Storage: Used to save your family recordings and the "Africa Progress Map."

5. Advertising

We show "G-rated" Rewarded Ads for non-premium users. These ads are filtered to be safe for children.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or using the "Clear Data" button in Settings.
